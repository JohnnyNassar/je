E-commerce platform

Joreption — Features & Pricing

A custom, bilingual (Arabic / English) online store with Cash-on-Delivery, built for the Jordanian market — replacing the WhatsApp selling group with a real platform.

What's included

🛍️ Customer storefront

  • Bilingual Arabic / English with full right-to-left support
  • Works on any phone; installable like an app (PWA)
  • Product catalog with search, categories & a featured strip
  • Product variations (color / size) — each with its own stock & price
  • Sale prices with "Save %" badges
  • Shopping cart & Cash-on-Delivery checkout (guest or account)
  • Discount / coupon codes
  • Loyalty points — customers earn & redeem for discounts
  • Order tracking & customer accounts
  • One-tap WhatsApp contact

⚙️ Admin & business tools

  • Dashboard with sales stats, charts & latest orders
  • Product management + mobile Quick Add (snap a photo, publish)
  • Order management with status workflow
  • Customers, categories, coupons & loyalty management
  • Media library for all product images
  • Staff accounts with limited (catalog-only) roles
  • Store settings: currency, branding, hero banner, "coming soon" mode
  • Notifications: in-app alerts now, email / SMS / WhatsApp ready to connect
  • Bulk import of products from a WhatsApp chat export
  • Built-in user guide

🔒 Hosting & technical (included in setup)

  • Dedicated cloud server (VPS)
  • Custom domain
  • HTTPS / SSL security
  • Daily database backups
  • Server security hardening & firewall
  • Fast, push-button deployment
